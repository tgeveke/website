About me

Background

Hi, I'm Tom! I'm a senior at Penn State studying mechanical engineering with a minor in engineering leadership development.
I have completed 2 internships at iRobot in Boston, MA as a test automation engineer, and 1 internship at Moog Aircraft Group in Buffalo, NY analyzing emerging technologies.
At Penn State, I am an undergraduate researcher with the CAIS Lab, conducting research on intelligent multi-robot systems. At the moment, we are working on vision systems for 3D print pick-and-place.
